Our story

Your Local Cleaning Experts in Dallas

To provide exceptional, eco-friendly cleaning services that make your home a healthier, happier place.

Dallas Shine Clean was founded in 2015 by a team of cleaning professionals who saw a need for reliable, high-quality house cleaning in Dallas. Starting with just two cleaners, we grew through word-of-mouth by consistently exceeding expectations. Today, we serve hundreds of homes across the city, from Lakewood to Uptown.

Our commitment to eco-friendly practices and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in Dallas. We invest in our team with ongoing training and fair wages, ensuring every cleaner is motivated to deliver their best. We're proud to be part of the Dallas community and to help our clients enjoy cleaner, healthier homes.
