Our story

Dallas's Trusted Cleaning Team

Our mission is to deliver exceptional cleaning services that give our clients more time to enjoy what matters most.

Sparkle & Shine was founded in 2014 by two Dallas neighbors who saw a need for reliable, eco-friendly house cleaning. Starting with just a bucket and a dream, we've grown to a team of 20+ trained professionals serving hundreds of homes across the city.

We believe a clean home is the foundation for a happy life. That's why we invest in our team, use only safe products, and stand behind every clean with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. When you choose Sparkle & Shine, you're choosing peace of mind.
